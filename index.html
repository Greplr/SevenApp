Greplr Hyperlocal Search

Greplr is exactly what you need to subscribe to a variety of services on one platform. This app will fulfill all your hyperlocal service search needs by allowing you to simultaneously discover and subscribe to the plethora of services like booking a cab, or buying a ticket that are available on a mobile platform. Greplr will eliminate the annoyance of switching between apps constantly and let you work out of 1 interface in order to do what you need to do by integrating key services for today’s market onto its brilliantly designed mobile application

Search, discover and do: Greplr is a hyperlocal search tool that will fulfill all your needs on a single platform. You can book cabs from Uber, TFS, and ola; search for a restaurant near you; book a bus ticket for your weekend getaway; a flight ticket for your honeymoon; or just read the news to kill some time. Greplr integrates all the products available on flipkart, amazon and snapdeal into one location letting you compare prices side by side and choose the best deal for you.
